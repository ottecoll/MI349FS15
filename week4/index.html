uno con el mundo

a dynamic blog bringing to light an array of environmental issues via the musings of an aspiring environmental journalist

“Uno con el mundo” will harness the wanderlust and transcendentalist spirit of an undergrad journalist applying her passion for visual communication to her respect for the environment. The blog will source Colleen Otte’s features from greatlakesecho.org and combine them with her personal musings, photographs and designs, providing her an outlet for advocating the preservation of the environment each and every one of us so heavily depends on.
